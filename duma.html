Dumas Beach is a beach along the Arabian Sea on the west coast of Gujarat state in India. It is located 21 km southwest of Surat and 265 km from Ahmedabad. Dumas Beach is known for its black sand and is a popular tourist destination for its scenic beauty and peaceful atmosphere.You can also eat the traditional food items such as Pav Bhaji, Bhajiya, Gathiya and others.
